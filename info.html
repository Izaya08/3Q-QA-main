Seeyah Corporation


"We are the Future."


Isaiah See, driven by his passion for personal computers and technology, founded a corporation in the year 2025. Isaiah's hobby of building and customizing PCs evolved into a full-fledged business venture, reflecting his deep interest and expertise in the field. His dedication to the craft and his vision for innovation led him to establish a company that would cater to the growing demand for high-performance, custom-built computers. The corporation, under Isaiah's leadership, aims to provide top-notch products and services to tech enthusiasts and professionals alike, ensuring that each customer's unique needs and preferences are met with precision and excellence. Through this venture, Isaiah See not only turned his hobby into a successful business but also contributed to the advancement of the technology industry.
